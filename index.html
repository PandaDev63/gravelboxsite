Gravel-BOX

Gameplay

Gravel-BOX is a sandbox game. You can do anything you want! Gravel-BOX let you summon many shapes to create anything.

Graphics

Gravel-BOX is all about shapes, 2d shapes! All the art is made of pixels. It also has a great art style.
